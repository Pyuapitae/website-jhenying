KAJITSU COOKIE

KAJITSU COOKIE - into the forest is designed to explore the value and memories of the Taiwanese land, incorporating our animals and colours. For this project I designed 3 unique packaging boxes for the cookie gift bundle, with the cookies inside matching the designed packaging.

Designed 2023

OATGOOD

OATGOOD is illustrated with layered brushstrokes and soft, earthy tones to evoke orchards, hills, and small farmhouses. Gentle greens and yellows create a warm, natural atmosphere that reflects the brand’s focus on purity and handcrafted care. A farmland‑shaped die‑cut window reveals the grains inside, connecting the illustration with the product and emphasizing OATGOOD’s commitment to honest, transparent ingredients.

Designed 2025

YANGMEI

YANGMEI uses simple, charming illustrations paired with red‑green linework to express the freshness of the local berry farms, giving consumers a sense of flavor, safety, and care. The packaging replaces the original plastic tray with a paper‑wrapped structure, adding refinement while avoiding excess materials. This approach protects the product while reflecting the brand’s thoughtful, considerate values.

Designed 2024

KAKUKA HANDMAKE

KAKUKA HANDMAKE creates handcrafted nut‑based pastries using local ingredients. This project focuses on developing a warm, customizable gift‑set identity that reflects the brand’s intention to bring people closer together through thoughtful, personal treats.

Designed 2018

EAT BREAD

EAT BREAD – packaging design for Italian bread balls and an in‑store takeaway bag, created to extend the brand’s warm, festive identity into both product and retail touchpoints. Both shopping bag and bread packing are designed with market drawn like graphic, with the special touch of the bread packaging wrapping around the bread instead of traditional plastic packing.

Designed 2021

LOTO BANANA BREAD

LOTO BANANA BREAD is a hand-made cake which is designed for sweets lovers who are diabetics or have low calorie needs. This cake is special as sugar-free production but the taste is as delicious as productions with sugars. In the package design, simple text layout and bright colors are also used to convey a simple and beautiful taste.

Designed 2018

ZIJI TAN TEA

ZIJI TANG TEA is built around the idea of “returning to oneself,” inviting tea‑drinking to become a calm, mindful pause in daily life. The identity uses simplicity, white space, and a logo shaped from the ideas of self, space, and tea. Warm wooden boxes and cream‑colored tins, finished with engraved and foiled details, create a gentle unboxing experience that reflects the brand’s quiet, attentive spirit.

Designed 2025

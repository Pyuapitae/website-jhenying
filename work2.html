OATGOOD OATGOOD

OATGOOD is illustrated with layered brushstrokes and soft, earthy tones to evoke orchards, hills, and small farmhouses. Gentle greens and yellows create a warm, natural atmosphere that reflects the brand’s focus on purity and handcrafted care.

Designed 2025

YANGMEI YANGMEI

YANGMEI uses simple, charming illustrations paired with red‑green linework to express the freshness of the local berry farms, giving consumers a sense of flavor, safety, and care.

Designed 2024

KAKUKA KAKUKA

EAT BREAD EAT BREAD
